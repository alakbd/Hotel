Hotel Description

Falcon Hotel on the west coast of Ireland in Galway city, near National University of Galway, St. Nicholas Collegiate Church and Eyre Square, Falcon hotel features free WiFi,free 24 hour wireless broadband Internet access and free private off street carpark and all our prices are including the tax in rate.

Our House Guest Accommodation can enjoy a full breakfast.

Eyre Square Shopping Centre, two sandy beaches, spectacular coastal walks, a variety of restaurants within walking distance, friendly traditional Irish music pubs, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and a variety of water sports .
